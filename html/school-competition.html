School Competition

Empowering students with healthy competition, talent discovery, and leadership skills.

CCA Department Inter-House Events Talent Discovery

Healthy Competition for Growth

We believe that all young people should have the opportunity to practice healthy competition. Thus, we have a separate CCA department, which conducts various competition to keep the students active.

Various Inter-House competitions will be held every month. The competitions are a fusion of talent, imagination, creativity and alertness.

The winners will be awarded with certificates during the school annual meet. Our competitions challenge the skills, inspire growth and provide the motivation to try hardest.
