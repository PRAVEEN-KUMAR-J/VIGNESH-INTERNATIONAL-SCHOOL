Chairman's Message

"Leading with Vision, Educating with Values"

Mr. R. Kuppusamy

Chairman

manikuppuswamy59@gmail.com

Dear Parents and Students,

I have immense pleasure in welcoming you to Vignesh International School, a highly progressive and enterprising school committed to provide quality education with great emphasis on moral values and discipline.

I firmly believe that education should promote confidence, discipline and clarity in thought and decision-making ability to set and achieve goals and above all, social responsibility as a life-long process. This must be fostered in the early years of life - childhood.

In order to achieve this, we @ Vignesh draw upon the best educational practices worldwide and create conditions for the children to imbibe strong moral values in a supportive environment. State-of-the-art infrastructure coupled with highly qualified and well-trained teachers will ensure that the students at VIS will be able to attain an enriching experience and high academic standards. I assure that the schooling in Vignesh is bound to be an exhilarating, overwhelming and memorable experience.

It is a matter of great pride to showcase the detailed information of the school and all its activities through the website. I take this opportunity to extend my best wishes to all the parents and students and look forward to cooperation and valuable suggestions from everyone for the betterment of our institution.

Warm Regards,

Mr. R. Kuppusamy

Chairman, Vignesh International School
