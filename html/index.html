Welcome to Vignesh International School

Transforming young minds since 2010

WELCOME

Vignesh International School is sponsored by Sree Selva Vinayagar Trust. The Trust was started in the year 1993, with a noble aim of imparting quality education to the rural public in and around Tiruvannamalai district.

Vignesh International School has been established to cater to the growing international demand for high quality education in the context of a rapidly globalizing educational scenario. The school is located in a serene atmosphere on Manalurpet Road at Melputhiyandal village in the holy town of Tiruvannamalai.

Our Infrastructure

World-class facilities to support holistic learning

Auditorium

A fully equipped auditorium is the venue for music, plays and addresses by eminent guest speakers.

Audio Visual Room

State-of-the-art AV room providing a grand ambience for small group discussions with parents & students.

Computer Lab

The lab has state-of-the-art Pentium computers with multimedia, internet and other modern technologies.

Transportation

Transport facilities are ensured for a radius of around 40 kms, prioritizing comfort and safety of children.

Hostel

Fully air-conditioned hostels for boys and girls separately, in a green and friendly environment.

Library

The main resource centre for students and faculty with a rich and diverse collection of books.

Dining & Catering

Mid-day meal with health drinks and snacks, with focus on health, hygiene and nutrition.

Science Labs

Three fully equipped science labs, each spread over more than 1,200 sq. ft. to support practical learning.
