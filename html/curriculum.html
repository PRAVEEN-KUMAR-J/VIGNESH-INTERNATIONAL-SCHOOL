Fast and Not So Fast Learners

We recognize and admit that there are individual differences among students. Some learn faster while some take a little more time to grasp a concept or proposition. We endeavor not to slow down the fast learner by encouraging him/her to learn at his/her normal speed, while at the same time the not-so-fast learners are paid additional attention and care to enhance his/her learning skills and understanding levels.

Examination & Assessment

Each child is assessed every term for understanding, skills, questioning, thinking and creativity. Parents will receive a full report of their child for each subject, every term. Further, students are assessed for their level and style of learning through exercises and discussions on each concept taught during the previous day/week. This helps the teacher in getting regular feedback and the student in getting practice of answering and solving problems.

Parent-Teacher Interaction

Parents can meet the Principal between 9:00 am and 4:00 pm by fixing an appointment with the office. Students can also meet the Principal in the office. There will be one parents meeting per term where parents can meet the teachers and discuss the progress of their children's work.

Constant and effective communication between parents and VIS is vital to the success of our educational programmes.
