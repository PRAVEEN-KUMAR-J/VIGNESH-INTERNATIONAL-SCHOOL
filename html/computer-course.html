Excellence in Technology

Computer courses are one of the best courses for students. There is an enormous increase of vigorous competition in every industry ranging from finance and engineering to technology. Getting a Certification course helps a student add a prestigious credential to the resume and stand ahead of others.

So, we at Vignesh are organising a certification programme called SUITS in collaboration with Bharathidasan University. The programme can equip a person to use the computer in day-to-day life for professional and personal use.
